Our Technology

Powered by Advanced AI

Our grape leaf disease detection system uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to accurately identify various diseases affecting grape plants.

Black Rot

Circular lesions with dark borders and tan centers on leaves. Fruit develops black rot.

Esca (Black Measles)

Tiger-stripe patterns on leaves with yellow or reddish-brown areas between green veins.

Leaf Blight

Small, irregular reddish-brown spots with yellow halos that can merge and cause defoliation.

Healthy Leaves

No visible symptoms. Leaves are green, intact, and free from spots or discoloration.
