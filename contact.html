Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting us

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, please call our support line directly.

Do you offer on-site consultations for vineyards?

Yes, we offer on-site consultations for commercial vineyards. Please contact us with details about your vineyard size and location for a custom quote.

Is there a mobile app available for GrapeGuard?

We're currently developing mobile applications for both iOS and Android. Join our mailing list to be notified when they become available.

Do you provide services internationally?

Yes, our disease detection technology is available worldwide. However, on-site consultations are currently limited to India and select international regions.
