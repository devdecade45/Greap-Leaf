Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our disease detection system

What image formats are supported?

Our system supports JPG, JPEG, and PNG image formats. For best results, use high-resolution images with good lighting and clear focus on the leaf.

How accurate is the detection?

Our model has been trained on thousands of images and achieves over 95% accuracy in controlled tests. However, accuracy may vary depending on image quality and disease severity. For critical cases, we recommend consulting with a viticulture expert.

Is my data kept private?

Yes, we respect your privacy. Uploaded images are only used for disease detection and are not shared with third parties. Images may be stored temporarily to improve our model's accuracy, but no personal information is collected or stored.

What if my grape variety isn't common?

Our model has been trained on various grape varieties, but performance may vary for rare varieties. We're continuously improving our model to cover more varieties. If you're working with an uncommon grape variety, please note this when interpreting results.
